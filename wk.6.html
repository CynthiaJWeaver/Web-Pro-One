Florida's BEST Standards

English Language Arts Grades 6-8

Benchmarks For Excellent Student Thinking. (Adopted February 2020)

In grades 6-8, analysis and writing are the primary foci. Rhetoric is introduced in 6th grade. In this grade band, students go from explaining theme to analyzing it. Students progress from examining character perspective to working with complex narrator types. Students are also being introduced to literature from historic time periods to help with building a body of knowledge useful in being able to interpret multiple layers of meaning.
