Florida's BEST Standards

English Language Arts Grades 6-8

Benchmarks For Excellent Student Thinking. (Adopted February 2020)

In grades 6-8, analysis and writing are of primary focus. Rhetoric is introduced in 6th grade. Students go from explaining theme to analyzing it. Students are also being introduced to literature from historic time periods.
